Community Service Activities

Here are some of the service events I've participated in. These experiences have helped me develop important skills while giving back to my community.

AMVETS Spanish Festival

During this event, I participated as a volunteer representing the Spanish Club. At the event, I helped clean trash for an hour with friends, as shown in the picture above. Additionally, I helped at the Spanish Club booth, creating Ojo Dios (God's eye) crafts which I distributed to children who attended. Through this experience, I learned to be fast with my hands, observant, and active.

Cultural Fest (WHS)

On this day, I was involved with the Spanish Club and Spanish Honor Society. During this event, I helped create arts and crafts for children. Because Day of the Dead was approaching, I created dye for sugar skulls. Through this event, I learned how to work well with others, be creative, and work efficiently.

Special Olympics

With the Leo club, I was formerly a member who helped out at Special Olympics. I helped guide children on the baseball field and encouraged participants. Being involved in this event taught me to be friendly, presentable, and encouraging.

St. Jude 25th Anniversary

At my church's 25th Anniversary celebration, I helped deliver food to elderly attendees and those seated. I also assisted with cleanup after the event, contributing to the success of this important milestone.
