Extracurricular Activities

The following carousel showcases groups and activities I've been involved with at school!

Chess Club

In Chess Club, I learned many new things about chess. I learned how to castle my rook & king, interpret chess puzzles, and read other opponents' moves. By being in chess, I developed qualities as a good listener, interpreter, and fast thinker.

Spanish Club

In the Spanish Club, I worked as our club's treasurer. With help from my class advisor, I learned how to manage our club funds, determine whether we could make a profit during school fairs, and how to write receipts for our club members. These experiences taught me to be fast, responsive, careful, and prepared.

Spanish Honor Society

In the Spanish Honor Society, I participated in many community service activities. These included bell ringing at Walmart to raise funds for Salvation Army, cleaning up for the safety fair, repainting sidewalks at Waipahu High School, and helping with the African American festival. Through these services, I learned to be attentive, hardworking, energetic, and involved in my community.

Tennis

I joined Waipahu High School's tennis program in 2022. Previously, I played tennis with my family as a hobby and was still a novice player, which placed me in 3rd doubles. When I joined the varsity team in my sophomore year, I improved significantly and won half of our games with my doubles partners. For the 2023-2024 season, I continued to improve and put in my best effort to win several games. Due to my hard work and character, I earned the Marauder award trophy.
