Spanish Club

In the Spanish Club, I worked as our club's treasurer. As a treasurer, I worked with help from my class advisor to learn how to manage our club funds, interpret whether or not we can make a profit during fairs we have at our schools, and how to write receipts to give out to our club members. By learning these skills by being the Spanish Club treasurer, I learned to be fast, responsive, careful, and prepared.

By attending these services, I learned to be attentive, hardworking, energetic, and involved in my community.

Tennis

Furthermore, for the 2023-2024, I still improved greatly and put all of my hard work in to win some of my games. By putting in my best efforts and by having a good character, I earned the Marauder award trophy for my hardwork and my character.
